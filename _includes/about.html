Welcome to Jersey City Chiropractic & Acupuncture

Established in 1987, Jersey City Chiropractic has been serving the Jersey City community for almost 30 years. Despite our recent name change, our goal has always remained the same – to help people live and function optimally. Our doctors utilize a whole-body approach to find the root cause of multiple health conditions. By combining ancient techniques with evidence-based protocols, our treatments are safe, effective, and customized for each patient. This holistic approach to healthcare is used to strengthen your body’s ability to heal naturally.
