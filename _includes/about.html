a little about us...

Established in 1987, Jersey City Chiropractic has been serving the Jersey City community for almost 30 years. Despite our recent name change, our goal has always remained the same – to help people live and function optimally. Our doctors utilize a whole-body approach to find the root cause of multiple health conditions. By combining ancient techniques from Chinese Medicine with evidence-based chiropractic protocols, our treatments are safe, effective, and customized based on each patient. This holistic approach to healthcare is used strengthen your body’s ability to heal naturally.
