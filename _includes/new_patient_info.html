NEW PATIENT INFO

What to expect on your first visit:

  1. PAPERWORK
    • Be prepared to spend 10-15 minutes filling out paperwork. You can always download the paperwork at home, print it, and bring it with you to your first visit.
    • Please bring your insurance card and a photo ID with you to your first visit.
    • Your first visit typically last one hour after completing your paperwork.
  2. DISCUSSION
    • After completing all paperwork, your doctor will spend one-on-one time with you to discuss all of the forms that you filled out.
    • You will be asked multiple questions regarding your condition as well as your general health and medical history.
    • If you are getting acupuncture, Dr. Dominique will look at your tongue and take your pulse.
  3. EXAM
    • Your doctor will then perform an exam by walking you through a series of tests.
    • Based on the results from these tests, your doctor will clearly identify the types of tissues (muscles, ligaments, bone, disc, organs) that are involved and diagnose your condition.
    • Some tests may increase your pain temporarily, however they will help your doctor understand what is causing your pain.
    • Depending on your condition, other diagnostic tests may be necessary, for example x-rays, MRIs, or laboratory tests including blood work.
  4. REVIEW
    • After your exam, your doctor will go over all of the results from the exam.
    • If chiropractic or acupuncture are appropriate for your condition, your doctor will discuss a recommended treatment plan and goals for care.
  5. TREATMENT
    • Lastly, depending on time, you will receive your first chiropractic or acupuncture treatment.
